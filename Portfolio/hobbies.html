Hobbies

Heres the page that tells what I like to do sometimes, every now and then.

Out of everything there is, I have a couple favorites to do during my free time or if I have the resources to do so.

  1. Paracording: I make a lot of bracelets, bag holders, lanyards, etc. I sell most at my local flea market on the weekends, but some say that I price them too high. But each to their own, gas is expensive. Especially when my only form of electricity is running on a generator.
  2. Art: I mostly do digital art, since it's more easier and there's more variety in the types of brushes that can be used. My common art that I do is planets, but with more practice as time goes on, I'll finally be able to draw concept art of my sketchbook drawings in their detailed glory. Such as arts like this:
  3. Woodworking: There's a lot of small woodworking projects that I've made over the years. From puzzles to step stools. My best one was building a paracord jig that can change sizes from bracelet size to rifle strap size.
  4. Gaming: I'm an advid gamer, I've played most games on various platforms. From console to PC gaming, it's all there. My favorite so far are physics-related games such as Teardown. There are others but I don't want to delve too deep down the rabbit hole of video games.
  5. Reading/Writing: I absolutely love to read. My favorite genres are from Action, Dystopian, Futuristic. These are my top three that I like to read. It's mostly series that I like to read like the Halo books, Dead Space books and comics, and published books that originally came from the online website Wattpad. I am currently in the process of writing my own book as well too. It's called Legacy, read it here. It's currently ongoing but I still update every now and then. I have a lot more of short stories within the Legacy universe but one thing to complete first before moving onto the next project.

And so there is all my hobbies. There are still things that I'd like to try out and see how they go. Just like paracording, I just wanted to try that out and see what I can make if I could make anything, and so I did make a lot of things. Now thats something I can make during my free time. I hope to learn more so I can create my own website to sell them to customers. That and sell my woodworking projects as well. This has been my description of hobbies that I have.
